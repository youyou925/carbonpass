About CarbonPass
CarbonPass is a carbon accounting and CBAM compliance SaaS platform built for Malaysian SME export manufacturers. We strictly follow the GHG Protocol Corporate Standard and EU CBAM Regulation (EU) 2023/956, using IPCC AR6 emission factors and Suruhanjaya Tenaga Malaysia grid factors, helping businesses accurately calculate emissions and generate compliant reports for the EU Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism.
